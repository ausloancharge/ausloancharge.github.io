Aussies against the Loan Charge

Retrospective law is evil -- all Australians deserve certainty in their affairs.

Foreign claims should only be raised for non contested tax charges - not tax in dispute (this includes APNs and the 2019 Loan charge)

We are a support group for Australians who are now caught up in the UK 2019 Loan Charge, working very closely with the Loan Charge Action Group.

If you worked in the UK in the last 20 years as a contractor and have received a brown envelope from HMRC, or a notice from the ATO, you should get in touch with us.

ATO foreign claim?

Some of those affected have begun to receive correspondence from the ATO for APNs issued by HMRC, for example:

Enclosed is a notice pursuant to subsection 263-30(2) of Schedule 1 to the Taxation Administration Act 1953 ("the Act"), notifying you of the particulars of a foreign revenue claim that has, pursuant to section 263-25 of Schedule 1 to the Act, been registered in the Foreign Revenue Claims Register.

If you have received anything from ATO like this, please contact us!
